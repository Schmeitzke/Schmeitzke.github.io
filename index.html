I am Camiel, a Data Science and
Artificial Intelligence student
at Maastricht University.

My academic journey began with my graduation from middle school, and I subsequently enrolled in Maastricht University to study fiscal economics. For two years, I delved into the intricacies of fiscal economics and law, finding them genuinely fascinating. However, as I looked toward the future, I couldn't envision myself pursuing a career in that field. In 2022, I decided to pivot and embrace a new academic path by enrolling in the Data Science and Artificial Intelligence program at Maastricht University. It was a decision I haven't regretted. The world of data science and AI captivated me from the start, and I've been fully dedicated to my studies ever since. Beyond academics, I actively engage with my local community. I'm proud to be a member of a foundation that organizes a variety of events throughout the year in my small village.
